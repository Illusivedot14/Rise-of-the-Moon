The story begins on a bustling port at Concordia, the capital of Armestria. The King, King Caelin, had just recently announced a joint effort with the Empire, to coordinate a rescue effort on the city of Sindral, a magical island city that has been engulfed by a storm for the last three months. One by one, each of the adventurers had boarded the S.S Sunshine, one of the ships that's taking part as part of the Beta group. The first of three groups of ships headed toward the island of Dral to evacuate the city.

Each member of the would-be party observed their surroundings to try to get a reading off of everyone else. Annaess, a magical detective, did not sign up for this task in the usual way, and boarded the ship as she was curious. Stumps came onto the ship, looking for some booze to drink while believing this was a paycheck. Buckamillion, a farmboy who had family at Sindral, ventured onto the ship to rescue their family. And Jana, who took this mission in place of her sister, wanting to prove herself.

Once the preparations had been set, the Captain, Jesse Marion, and Ron Starfallen, both debrief the party and assigned them to arbitrary groups of four for the effort.

Each of the expeditions had a specific focus: Beta was going through the south entry point of the island, Gamma was entering from the west towards the low-lands, and Delta was headed toward the more military side of the island. The goal was to reach the center of the island, a tall building called the Shard, and to coordinate with the first rescue group, Alpha, that was sent when the storm was beginning.

And so, after going to the mess hall to drink some suspicious drinks from the beer enthusiast, the ship sets sail. The ship sails for about half a day, reaching the storm wall. The current bashes against all the ships as they all stand by for the King's ship to take the lead. The party takes to the deck, while spotting a small black ship sailing forward past the line of ships. The King's ship eclipses the black ship, taking the lead. After his assistant brought something out in a cage, covered in a white cloth, the King channel some sort of energy resembling an aurora borealis to lift a portion of the storm, allowing the ships to sail inside.

Once inside, the air is still as the party sees a calm and serene sight. There is no island of Dral, just the flat water. As the ships continue to sail further inside, Annaess spots fish creatures climbing up the side of the deck. The party fights as the crew runs inside for cover, locking them out. The rest of the ships begin to fight as well. After the first wave of fish creatures, a large fish creature with its warlock friend climb up, threatening to throw the party overboard, but after a few close calls and a clutch heal, the party survives.

As soon as they recover, they notice the rest of the ships from the other expeditions are gone. When the realization hits their heads, a Kraken submerges the ship, breaking it into two and dragging the party underwater.

As the party wakes, they realize that they're in an underwater air pocket, with the entire city submerged. And all they hear in the distance:

> ..and to all those who are just joining us, may I be the first to say, Welcome to Sindral.
